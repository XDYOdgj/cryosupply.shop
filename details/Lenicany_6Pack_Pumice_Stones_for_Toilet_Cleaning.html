Lenicany 6Pack Pumice Stones

The Lenicany 6Pack Pumice Stones are an excellent choice for anyone looking for a natural and effective way to keep their bathroom and kitchen surfaces clean and shiny. These eco-friendly stones are made from recycled glass, making them a sustainable alternative to harsh chemical cleaners. Their soft yet durable surface is perfect for removing stubborn stains, limescale, and hard water rings without scratching or damaging your fixtures.

Ideal for use on toilets, bathtubs, kitchen sinks, grills, and ovens, these pumice stones offer versatility and value. Just wet the stone, test it on a small area, and scrub in a circular motion to lift away grime and dirt easily. Because they are reusable and long-lasting, they provide an economical solution for all your cleaning needs. Whether you're tackling calcium buildup or everyday household mess, these stones are designed to deliver great results with minimal effort.
